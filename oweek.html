Orientation Week

O-Week is run
by your classmates.

Every August, an O-Week team — drawn from the year above you — runs the orientation experience for the incoming class. Schedule, events, social mixers, the whole thing. They have their own site, their own communications, and they're often the first MD students you'll meet.

Run by upper-year students The O-Week team plans and executes orientation independently. They are your most direct line to "what happens next" in August.
Watch your inbox O-Week registration, RSVP forms, and event invites come from the team's account directly — not from the Faculty.
The Site

The O-Week site is the source of truth for all orientation events.

The full schedule, RSVP forms, dress codes, location pins, last-minute changes, and the team's bios all live on the dedicated O-Week site, maintained by the team. Bookmark it the moment you receive your offer.

The site is refreshed each year by the incoming O-Week team. The link above goes to the current year's site.

Backpack day. The class of 2T9.

170 photos from the first morning of O-Week — the first thing every incoming med student picks up. Hover over any row to pause it. Click a photo to expand.

Welcome Picnic at Christie Pits.

5:00 — 7:00 PM · Dinner provided

The cohort heads west to Christie Pits Park as the official welcome wraps. Free dinner, sun on the lawn, the first time most of the class meets each other in casual clothes.

02

Tuesday.

Aug 19, 2025

Dean's Tea at Hart House.

10:00 AM — 12:00 PM · Refreshments

A formal welcome from the Faculty inside Hart House. Tea, a few speeches, and the first chance to meet faculty in person.

MED Olympics on Front Campus.

1:30 — 4:00 PM · Front Campus Field

Team-based outdoor games on the lawn between buildings. Egg-and-spoon, tug-of-war, the kind of organized chaos that gets people who haven't talked yet to talk.

03

Wednesday.

Aug 20, 2025

Boat Formal on Lake Ontario.

6:30 — 9:30 PM · 207 Queens Quay W · Dinner provided

A formal dinner cruise on the harbour as the sun goes down behind the city skyline. The first time the cohort dresses up for itself.

Awaiting photos · 10–15 expected. Send Boat Formal photos to wire into this spread. Layout will adjust to actual photo count and orientation.

Board Game Night.

6:30 — 10:00 PM · MSB 2158 Cafeteria · Dinner provided

Team Blast: Survival Guide and board games to close out the week. Dinner's on us — come hungry, leave knowing your team.

05

Friday.

Aug 22, 2025

Academy Day.

8:30 AM — 4:30 PM · At your assigned academy

Each student spends Friday at their assigned academy meeting faculty, touring the home hospital, and getting a feel for the building they'll spend most of Foundations in. The cohort splits across the four academies — FitzGerald, Peters-Boyd, Mississauga, and Wightman-Berris.

Stethoscope Ceremony at Convocation Hall.

5:00 — 8:30 PM · Doors 4:45 PM, start 6:00 PM

The closing ceremony of O-Week. Each student receives their first stethoscope under the dome of Convocation Hall — the formal start of medical school.
